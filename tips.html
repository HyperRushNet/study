Study Tips

Plan your study sessions and create a clear schedule.

Use the Pomodoro technique: 25 minutes of focus, 5 minutes of rest.

Summarize material in your own words.

Review key information regularly to improve memory.

Test yourself with practice questions or flashcards.

Find a quiet, well-lit place to study.

Keep your phone away to avoid distractions.

Use colors and diagrams to structure information.

Learn actively by asking questions and making connections.

Sleep well, good rest improves memory.

Alternate theory with practice to stay engaged.

Discuss difficult topics with classmates.

Use online resources such as videos or interactive tools.

Reward yourself after productive sessions.

Stay hydrated and eat healthy for better concentration.
